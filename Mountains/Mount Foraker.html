Where is Mount Foraker

Mount Foraker is located in the Alaska Range in the state of Alaska, USA

More Information

Mount Foraker is known for its stunning, rugged beauty.

Fun Facts

Height: Mount Foraker stands at 20,310 feet (6,190 meters), making it the third tallest peak in North America.

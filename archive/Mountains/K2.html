All About K2 ⛰️

Where is K2

K2 Is located in the Karakoram Range. It lies on the border between Pakistan and China

More Information

K2 is the second-highest mountain in the world.

Fun Facts

Height: K2 stands at 28,251 feet (8,611 meters) above sea level, making it the second-highest mountain in the world. Names: It's also known as Mount Godwin-Austen, named after the surveyor Henry Godwin-Austen, though this name is less commonly used.
